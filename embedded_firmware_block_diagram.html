Module Notes

Core/Src/main.c

Owns boot sequencing, the infinite loop, face state, touch handling, framed BLE protocol parsing, and the handoff between transport, display, and audio subsystems.

Core/Src/at09_ble.c

Converts a single-byte UART interrupt stream into higher-level buffers for raw commands, full video frames, and fixed-size audio packets, while also mirroring traffic to USART2.

Core/Src/audio_packet.c

Validates incoming audio packets, decodes ADPCM into PCM samples, stores them in a ring buffer, and feeds the DAC from the TIM6 interrupt. It also loops the last packet when no new audio arrives.

Core/Src/sh1106.c

Owns the display RAM buffer and converts drawing operations or raw frame writes into SH1106 I2C transfers over I2C2.
